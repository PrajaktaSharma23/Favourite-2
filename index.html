My Favourite Hobby
Drawing

My favourite Hooby is drawing since i was in standard one. I like it because for a simple drawing you do not need much materials until you are going to make a realistic or animatic drawing. Even when you see your drawings it delights you and gives you a feeling that I can do it too. I usually draw on ibispaint these days and sometimes I like to draw in a paper itself.
